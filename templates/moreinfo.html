Meteorite Landing Visualizations

    ~Meteorites are divided into 3 overall categories.
    ~1. Rocky Material (stony meteorites)
    ~2. Metallic Material (iron meteorites)
    ~3. Hybrid (stony-iron meteorites)
    ~Meteorites in the 'Iron' recclass signify the heavier category.
    ~L6, H5, L5, H6, H4, LL5, LL6, L4, H4/5, CM2 are the most frequent classes of Meteorites Landings.
    ~The data indicates that heavy meteorite hits are rare.
    ~Lightweight categories like L6, H5, L5, etc., are more commonly observed.
